    GLOBAL SERVICES HUB APPLICATION

    GS HUB

    Is a safe and secure platform which is available worldwide for Subject Matter Experts (SME) to create and display their professional profiles online. Services Project Owners (SPO) will also upload Decentralized Task Orders (DTO) on the same platform and the SMEs can bid competitively for the DTOs, leading to good value for money and reduced services costs for the Services Project Owners (SPOs). In turn the platform affords opportunities to professionals all over the world to offer services on a “freelance” basis while working from any geographical location of their choice.

    Global Investment Hub Application

    GI HUB

    A global platform that provides a safe and secure online system which is available to government institutions, private companies, academic institutions, and individual entrepreneurs (known as Investment Project Owners (IPO)) to post their projects seeking investments and financing partnerships across the globe. The projects are viewed by potential investors and funding partners from all over the world who then competitively bid for the opportunities to secure equity in the projects in exchange for funding.

      Global Project Management Hub Application

      GPM HUB

      A global project management application that allows the DBS Ecosystem participants to breakdown their larger and more complex projects and/ or business processes into small, discrete, executable, and quantifiable steps or job tasks called Project Management Tasks (PmT).

      Global Fiat & Cryptocurrency Exchange Hub Application

      FCX HUB

      A global currency exchange platform that will support the DBS Ecosystem participants all over the world to convert their hard-earned money to any fiat or cryptocurrency of their choice. It will particularly serve the unbanked masses in developing countries where people are exposed to hyperinflation, corrupt government controlled central banks and where Central Bank Digital Currencies to not exist. DBS Ecosystem participants in these geographical locations will make use of the TasKoin (T$K); a low level digital stable coin that will be exchangeable at a rate of one-to-one with the local fiat currency. This will then in turn be frictionlessly converted to the BisKoin (B$K) token which they can.

        Global Decarbonisation & Green Certificates Exchange Hub Application

        GCX HUB

        The DBS Ecosystem participants can receive free BisKoins (B$K) through Carbon Emission (tCO2e) Mining; a process where a participant organisation or individual is issued BisKoins (B$K) in exchange for reducing carbon emissions or equivalent in clean energy generation, in exchange for green certificates. This function will prioritize projects in developing regions where it can also be used as an investment securitization strategy.
